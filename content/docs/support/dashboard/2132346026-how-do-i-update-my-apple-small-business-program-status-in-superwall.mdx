---
title: "How do I update my Apple Small Business Program status in Superwall?"
---


Context
-------

When participating in Apple's Small Business Program, your proceeds cut is 15% instead of the standard 30%. To ensure your Superwall dashboard reflects the correct revenue calculations, you'll need to configure your program status in the settings.

Answer
------

To update your Apple Small Business Program status in Superwall:

![Apple Small Business Program](/images/apple-small-business-program.png)

1.  Navigate to [Settings/General](https://superwall.com/applications/:app/settings) in your Superwall dashboard
    
2.  Locate the Apple Small Business Program section
    
3.  Enter your program start date, and if applicable, exit date
    
4.  Save your changes
    

Note: The updated proceeds cut calculation will only apply to transactions processed after you've saved these settings. Historical transactions will not be recalculated.
