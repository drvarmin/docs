title: "Products Not Loading: Troubleshooting Guide"
---

Products not loading is a common issue when first setting up any subscription app. This is the starting point for Superwall's comprehensive guide to troubleshooting this issue.

## Symptoms
- Prices not showing on paywall
- Tapping purchase button on paywall doesn't do anything
- Error logs saying products not loading

## Guides
Depending on where you are seeing this issue, can change how to debug a lot. Please check the guide for your device type:

- [iOS (real device)](/support/troubleshooting/products-not-loading/1253018505-products-not-loading-ios)
